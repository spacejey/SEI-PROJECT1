HUNGRY SNAKE

RULES

1. Hit the start button!

2. Use the arrow keys to move the snake's head!

3. Eat your prey! If you eat it, your tail will grow long. Scores will accumulate!

4. If you hit the wall,
Game Over!

TIPS

Every 5 pieces of food you eat increases your speed!
